Projects

Aawaz-e-Dil Podcast

As part of my journey in content creation, I launched a podcast called Aawaz-e-Dil, where I host conversations with inspiring individuals, entrepreneurs, and thought leaders across various fields. The podcast is focused on soul-stirring discussions, personal growth, and the intersection of technology and entrepreneurship.

The first episode, featuring Anand Dwivedi, a renowned social media personality with deep insights into meditation and mindfulness, was an incredible success. Anand Dwivedi has a massive following with around 80K subscribers on YouTube and 168K followers on Instagram, bringing his profound expertise to the audience. The episode garnered over 40K views on Instagram and established a strong base of listeners for future episodes. The conversation covered various aspects of personal growth, the power of meditation, and how it can be a game-changer for entrepreneurs in terms of mental clarity and focus.

The podcast aims to create a platform for sharing authentic and insightful stories that inspire others. Each episode seeks to bring new perspectives on entrepreneurship, self-development, and navigating the challenges of building a successful career in the digital world. I’m excited about continuing to grow this podcast and collaborating with more incredible guests in the future.
